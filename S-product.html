Spiral Binding

Product Details

Spiral binding is a binding method that involves punching holes along one edge of a document and inserting a plastic or metal coil through the holes. It is a durable and cost-effective binding method that allows for pages to lay flat and can be opened 360 degrees. Spiral binding can be done manually or with the help of a machine and is available in a variety of sizes and colors. It is a popular choice for manuals, cookbooks, and other reference materials.
